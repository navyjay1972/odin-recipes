Mac & Cheese

Description

Basic baked macaroni and cheese recipe

One of our favorites

  • 1 1/2 cup of Macaroni
  • 3 tbsp of Butter
  • 2 tbsp Flour
  • Dash of Salt and Pepper
  • 8oz of Sharp Cheddar Cheese
  • 2 cups of Milk
  1. Cook Macaroni
  2. Combine other ingredients and stir until cheese is melted
  3. Pour cheese sauce over macaroni in casserole dish
  4. Bake for 1 hour at 350F
